Who We Are

Monera builds AI-native software shaped around the deep logic of a sector — so the intelligence is already fluent in your world the moment it arrives.

Most software is rebuilt from zero for every client — the same problems solved again and again, slower and more expensively each time. We took the opposite path.

We engineer once, for an entire industry. Our platforms encode the workflows, language, and edge-cases of a sector into adaptive AI systems that configure themselves to each operator — without starting over.

The result is software that feels custom, but carries the accumulated intelligence of a whole field behind it.
