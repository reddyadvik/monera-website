What it does

The entire proposal
workflow, automated

Pharma CROs spend weeks on every proposal — coordinating across BD, R&D, Manufacturing, Quality, Supply Chain, and EHS just to get a costing together. ProposalHub encodes that entire workflow into one AI-native system.

The AI drafts the proposal based on the study type and requirements. Each department reviews their section, fills in time and cost, and signs off sequentially. No email chains. No Excel. No chasing people.

Every proposal is tracked, every turnaround is measured, and the entire institutional knowledge lives in one place — not in individual inboxes.
